Relive the magic from one of Disney’s most iconic films with this new bone china ‘Dreams and Wishes’ Bambi mug.

This beautiful mug features an original sketch from the classic film Bambi, with the sentiment ‘Dreams and Wishes’ in gold foil lettering.
